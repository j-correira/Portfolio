About

I'm a dynamic IT professional who is deeply passionate about software engineering and graphic design. I have a creative outlook on problem solving coupled with a lifelong desire to learn and absorb information.


I currently hold two Associate’s Degrees from New England Institute of Technology, in Graphic/Web Design and Software Engineering. The combination of these two degrees and my previous internship experience, grant me a dynamic and valuable set of skills.
